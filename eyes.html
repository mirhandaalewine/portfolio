Givin' Me Googly Eyes


Googly Eyes: the emotional support you never knew you needed!

The Googly Eyes project uses simple CSS and JavaScript to create two eyeballs with responsive pupils. Both pupils move at the speed and in the direction of the user's mouse. Never miss an opportunity to roll your eyes again.
