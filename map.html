Mappin' It Out in Boston


Take a trip through the city using a Real Time Bus Tracker!

The Bus Tracker project uses the Massachusetts Transport Authority's API to track the real-time locations of busses on the route from MIT to Harvard. Users can watch as the location of each bus updates every 15 seconds.
